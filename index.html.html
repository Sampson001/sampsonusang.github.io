Research Work

FINAL YEAR PROJECT

🔬
Performance Characteristics of Polymer Matrix Reinforced with Jute Fibre for Ceiling Board Material
Solo Research · FUTMINNA · 2025
Conducted independent research investigating the use of jute fibre and epoxy resin as a sustainable composite material for ceiling board manufacturing. Designed and executed experiments to test three key mechanical properties: tensile strength, hardness, and impact resistance.

Results were consistent with conventional ceiling board materials, leading to a formal recommendation that jute fibre-reinforced polymer composites represent a viable and sustainable alternative for ceiling board production.
🧪
Materials Used: Jute fibre + Epoxy resin composite
📊
Tests Conducted: Tensile strength, Hardness, Impact resistance
✅
Result: Properties consistent with conventional ceiling board materials
💡
Conclusion: Recommended jute fibre composite as a sustainable ceiling board alternative
